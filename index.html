GAME MECHANICS:

  • HP stands for hit points (health).
  • SP stands for soul points. SP is connected to dimensionality, hence a zero-dimensional being would have zero SP whereas a one-dimensional being would have SP greater than zero.
  • Note: SP can NOT be attacked until an enemy's HP is reduced by at least 50%
  • GRIT can be thought of as stamina. Taking actions requires GRIT. If GRIT decreases to zero, a character's next turn will be a forced rest (turn will be skipped).
  • BLOCK strengthens defense on the opponent's next turn and goes away after one turn.
  • BUFF strengthens offense on the player's next turn and goes away after one turn.
SECOND PAGE

ACTIONS EXPLAINED:

  • Bash is an offensive move that decreases enemy HP.
  • Soul Compress will reduce enemy SP on the Y-axis (decrease height). Soul Compress is disabled until enemy HP is reduced by at least 50%.
  • Rest heals HP and recovers GRIT. Max values cannot be exceeded.

HOW TO WIN:

  • You can win by either killing the enemy or be reducing their existence to zero dimensions.
  • Clearly one way is easier than the other.
  • What will you do?
